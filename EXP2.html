About PDEU

Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU) is a premier institute dedicated to providing excellence in education, research, and innovation in the fields of energy, engineering, and business management.

Departments

  • School of Technology: Focus on AI, Data Science, and Renewable Energy.
  • School of Business: Specializing in Management, Marketing, and Finance.
  • School of Liberal Studies: Offering courses in Social Sciences and Humanities.

Courses

  • B.Tech: Mechanical, Electrical, Computer Science, and more.
  • MBA: Business Analytics, Finance, and HR.
  • Ph.D. Programs: Cutting-edge research opportunities.
